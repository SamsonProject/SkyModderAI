What we collect

We store account data you provide (email, password hash if set), session metadata for security, and content you submit in community posts or developer API key labels.

What we do not collect

We do not process card numbers directly. Stripe handles payment details. We also avoid logging raw personal data where possible.

How data is used

Data is used to operate core features: account access, load order analysis, subscription status, saved settings, and community interactions.

OpenClaw Lab policy acknowledgements may be stored or logged for safety and audit purposes.

Retention and security

We use secure cookies and basic security headers in production. You can revoke active sessions from your profile at any time.

Safety and policy acknowledgements for high-risk features may be retained for operational accountability and abuse prevention.

Third parties

Stripe is used for billing. Optional integrations like Google Sign-In are only used if enabled by deployment settings.

If OpenClaw Lab is enabled, operations are intended to stay confined to a dedicated workspace folder and explicit consent flow.
