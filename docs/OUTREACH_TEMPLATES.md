## What Is SkyModderAI?

SkyModderAI is a free, open-source tool that analyzes your mod list and detects conflicts before they break your game.

**Key features:**
- **Conflict Detection:** Finds incompatible mods using LOOT rules plus community knowledge
- **Smart Recommendations:** Suggests specific patches from Nexus Mods
- **Manager Integration:** Works with Mod Organizer 2, Vortex, and Wabbajack
- **Privacy First:** Runs locally, your mod list stays private
- **100% Free:** No accounts, no paywalls, no BS

---

## How It Works

### Step 1: Export Your Mod List
From your mod manager, export your active mods (takes 10 seconds).

### Step 2: Analyze
Paste your list into SkyModderAI and click "Analyze."

### Step 3: Review Results
You'll see:
- ‚úÖ Compatible mods (no action needed)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Conflicts (need patches or load order changes)
- ‚ùå Incompatible mods (can't use together)

### Step 4: Apply Fixes
Click on each conflict for specific solutions. Download recommended patches. Re-analyze to confirm.
